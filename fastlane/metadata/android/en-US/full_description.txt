Add custom text to any image with a fully accessible, privacy-first editor. ImageType was designed from the ground up for blind and sighted users alike — clean interface, zero clutter, works entirely on your device.

PERFECT FOR

* Greeting cards for holidays and special occasions.
* Social media posts, quotes, and promotional content.
* WhatsApp, Facebook, and Instagram stories.
* Arabic calligraphy, Quranic verses, and poetry on custom backgrounds.
* YouTube and Reels thumbnail text overlays.

ACCESSIBILITY

* Built for TalkBack from day one — every button, slider, and menu is properly labeled.
* Sliders announce their current value. Checkboxes read "On" and "Off."
* Live region announcements for image generation status.
* Available in Arabic, English, French, Italian, Spanish, German, and Turkish.

TEXT & FONTS

* Type in any language — mix RTL Arabic with LTR English on a single image. Full emoji support included.
* Cairo and Amiri bundled for instant use. System fonts like Roboto available out of the box. Additional fonts (Open Sans, Montserrat, Lato, Oswald, Raleway, Noto Sans Arabic) download on first use and cache locally.
* Auto Fit Text calculates the largest possible font size to fill your image — one checkbox, instant result.
* Nine position anchors, adjustable size, Bold and Italic styles, fifteen text and background colors.

CANVAS & BACKGROUNDS

* Eight preset dimensions: Standard HD, Square, Portrait/Reels, Ultrawide, Twitter Header, and more.
* Transparent background support for overlay graphics.
* Pick any photo from your gallery through Android's built-in Photo Picker — no blanket storage access.

PRODUCTIVITY

* Save and load custom templates. Switch between designs instantly.
* Share text from other apps via the Android Share menu, and share the final image directly without saving to your gallery.

PRIVACY

* No data collection. No telemetry, no analytics, no crash reports.
* No tracking, no ads, no remote servers.
* INTERNET permission is used only to download extra font files from Google Fonts. Fonts cache locally after the first download.
* Fully offline for every core feature. Your images and text never leave your device.
